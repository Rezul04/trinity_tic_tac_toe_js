Trinity Tic-Tac-Toe

Game Setup

  • There are three Tic Tac Toe grids (3x3 each).
  • Only the symbol "X" is used by both players

Game Play

  • Players take turns placing one "X" on any of the three grids.
  • Each player can only place one "X" per turn.
  • Players can place their "X" on any of the available spots on any of the three grids.

Blocking Grids

  • If a player gets three "X"s consecutively (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) in a grid, that grid is blocked.
  • Blocked grid cannot be used for the rest of the game.
  • Once a grid is blocked, no more "X"s can be placed on it.

Endgame

  • If two of the three grids are blocked, players can only play on the last remaining grid.
  • The game ends when a player places an "X" that results in three consecutive "X"s in the last unblocked grid.
  • The player who places the last "X" that completes a row of three "X"s in the final grid loses the game, and the other player is declared the winner.
